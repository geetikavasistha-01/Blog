Privacy Safeguards

This blog uses a self-hosted analytics mechanism running locally in SQLite. It respects your visitors' privacy:

  • No Third-Party Trackers: No requests are made to external analytics services (like Google Analytics).
  • No Persistent Cookies: Cookies are strictly restricted to admin sessions; visitors are tracked cookie-free.
  • No Raw IP Logs: Visitor identifiers are securely hashed with a rotating daily salt. It is impossible to reverse-engineer raw IP addresses or track users across days.
